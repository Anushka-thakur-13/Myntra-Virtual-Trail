Myntra Virtual Trial: FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Myntra Virtual Trial is an innovative platform that revolutionizes fashion with its virtual try-on technology. You can upload your picture and any image of clothes you want to try out, enabling you to virtually try on clothes online as if you were physically trying them on in front of a mirror. It supports upper wear clothes for men and women.

Myntra Virtual Trial can be used by individuals to virtually try on clothes from any image found online, including celebrity outfits or random fashion items. It's ideal for online shoppers looking to see how clothes look before buying. Additionally, online stores can integrate this tool to provide a virtual try-on feature for their customers.

Myntra Virtual Trial works by extracting the clothing item from the source image and fitting it onto your uploaded profile image. It employs two separate deep learning models: a cloth segmentation model to extract the clothing and a pose estimator model to locate shoulder points. Finally, the extracted clothing is seamlessly blended into your profile image based on the shoulder location.
